About Lumen Tekno

Your trusted partner for all electrical appliance needs

Lumen Tekno is a trusted provider of electrical goods and comprehensive customer support services. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide quality appliances at competitive prices while delivering exceptional customer service that goes above and beyond expectations.

We understand that electrical appliances are essential for modern living, whether it's keeping your family comfortable with efficient air conditioning, preserving food with reliable refrigeration, or enjoying entertainment with the latest LED technology. That's why we've built a business model focused on quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction.

Our team consists of highly trained professionals with extensive experience in the electrical goods industry. From our sales consultants who help you choose the perfect appliance for your needs, to our installation technicians who ensure everything is set up correctly, to our service engineers who provide prompt and reliable repair services, every member of our team is dedicated to your satisfaction.

We've built strong relationships with leading manufacturers and suppliers, allowing us to offer a wide range of products at competitive prices. Whether you're looking for budget-friendly options or premium brands, we have something to suit every need and budget. Our commitment to quality means we only stock products from reputable manufacturers with proven track records of reliability and performance.

What truly sets us apart is our comprehensive approach to customer service. We don't just sell appliances - we provide complete solutions. From the moment you walk into our showroom or contact us online, through the installation process, and for the entire lifespan of your appliance, we're here to support you. Our 24/7 service availability ensures that help is always just a phone call away, no matter when you need assistance.

We take pride in our commitment to customer satisfaction and environmental responsibility. All our products meet the highest energy efficiency standards, helping you save on electricity bills while reducing your carbon footprint. Our installation services are designed to minimize waste and ensure optimal performance from day one. When it comes to repairs, we prioritize fixing rather than replacing, extending the life of your appliances and reducing electronic waste.

Our success is built on the trust and loyalty of our customers. We've helped thousands of families and businesses find the perfect appliances for their needs. Our customers return to us for their electrical goods needs, which speaks volumes about the quality of our products and service. We're a trusted partner in your home and business.
